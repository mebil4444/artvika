Workshop

Paper Flower Workshop

Create long-lasting blooms using premium crepe paper. Learn petal shaping, curling, coloring, wiring, and bouquet assembly.

  • Duration: 2.5–3 hours
  • Group size: 6–10 participants
  • All materials included
  • Location: Maura / Oslo area
Book this workshop See upcoming dates

What you’ll make

One complete stem (rose/peony) + fillers, assembled into a mini bouquet to take home.

What you’ll learn

  • Petal templates & cutting
  • Stretching & curling techniques
  • Coloring & blending
  • Wiring & assembly

Who is it for?

Perfect for beginners. No prior experience required.
